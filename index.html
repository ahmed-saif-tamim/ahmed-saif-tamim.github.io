About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Biological Sciences working on genome regulation, chromatin biology, and 3D genome architecture. My research includes genomics and chromatin profiling by high-throughput sequencing, live-cell single-molecule imaging, and sequential DNA-FISH to study spatial genome organization and nuclear structure. My work also involves the use of AI alongside these approaches.

Outside the lab, I enjoy traveling, watching anime, and practicing mindfulness. I value quiet focus, curiosity, and steady progress in both research and life.
